Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Overview

We value your privacy and strive to be transparent about the data we collect and how we use it. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected by PokerNow Master (the "Extension"), how it is used, and your choices regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address and unique user ID collected via Firebase Authentication.
  • Usage Metrics: Number of hands played and subscription metadata stored in Firestore.
  • Payment Data: Subscription status and billing events managed by Stripe; we do not store payment card details.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to authenticate you, provide premium features, calculate subscription status, and deliver product updates.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted third-party processors:

  • Firebase – authentication, database, and hosting
  • Stripe – subscription billing

5. Data Retention

Account and gameplay data is retained as long as your account remains active or as needed to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

6. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest within Firebase and Stripe. Access is limited to authorized personnel.

7. Cookies

The Extension itself does not use cookies. Firebase may set secure cookies for authentication purposes.

8. Your Choices & Rights

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
